## Key Numbers at a Glance

### Calorie Targets (Ultra Running)
- **Elite finishers**: ~333 cal/hour, ~83g carbs/hour
- **General finishers (100 mi)**: 250–300 cal/hour, ~70g carbs/hour
- **Non-finishers**: <200 cal/hour, <45g carbs/hour
- **Recommended range**: 150–350 cal/hour depending on effort and duration

### Calorie Density by Category (Cal/oz, as carried)
- Oils: ~240 cal/oz
- Nuts / nut butters: 160–205 cal/oz
- Dry snacks (chips, crackers): 109–154 cal/oz
- Energy bars: 100–142 cal/oz
- Freeze-dried meals (dry): 95–155 cal/oz
- Energy gels: 41–114 cal/oz (varies widely)
- Energy chews: 88–95 cal/oz
- Drink mix powders: 85–124 cal/oz
- Real food (cooked/fresh): 5–95 cal/oz

### Data Quality Notes
- **Spring Energy**: Label accuracy is disputed; lab testing found <50% of claimed calories in some products. Flag in app.
- **Freeze-dried meals**: Densities listed are **dry weight only** (what is carried). Add water on site.
- **Drink mixes**: Densities listed are **dry powder weight** (what is carried).
