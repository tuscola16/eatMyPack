Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2025

eatMyPack ("the app", "we", "us") is a race nutrition planning tool for ultra and trail runners. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account information. If you create an account, we collect your email address and, if you sign in with Google, your name and profile photo as provided by Google.
  • Race and nutrition data. Race configurations (distance, duration, conditions), food preferences, pantry selections, and generated pack plans you save.
  • Crash and error data. If the app crashes or encounters an error, diagnostic information (device type, OS version, app version, and a snapshot of app state at the time of the error) is sent to Sentry (sentry.io) to help us fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is intentionally included in these reports.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your race plans and preferences across devices when you are signed in.
  • To improve app stability using anonymized crash reports.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Account data and saved plans are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in the United States. If you use the app without an account, all data is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — error monitoring. Sentry Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication method. Google Privacy Policy

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen within the app. Local device data can be cleared by uninstalling the app. Crash reports are retained by Sentry for up to 90 days.

Children's Privacy

eatMyPack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
